Kiwi Summer Ale

ABV: 4.1%

Brewer: Stuart Lawton

Style: NZ Ale

A riff on 'monteiths summer ale' brewed in Greymouth, NZ. As is typical with a NZ 'ale', a Pilsner malt base is used. This is supplemented with taiheke hops, fresh ginger, orange peel and rata honey (Rata is known as the New Zealand Christmas tree). This creates a crystal clear, summer beer - perfect for a beer or 2 out in the sunshine.
